ABOUT ME

Learn about my

musings, character, and career.

I’m a Manila-based software developer that has a full-stack background and love for good design. I’ve worked with startups since my college internship period and has experienced developing solutions for various business areas such as events management, human resources, online learning, travel insurance, and business finance.

During my college years, I haven’t considered a software engineering career and was really aiming for a more design-focus role. Then, a string of events in my life happened and I ended up having fun from coding user interface interactions using C# WinForms up to writing PHP in Laravel for our Capstone project. When the internship term came, I’ve discovered the world of startups and after doing some research, I settled and narrowed down my applications for early-stage companies only. And after being accepted by one, I thought that it was a great decision since I get to wear different hats and just be a sponge that absorbs every knowledge around me.

Now, I still keep the same growth mindset and love for the startup ecosystem that I picked up on the first year of my career. Moving forward, I learned how to make my work more fun by attending community events, doing public speaking (both internally and externally), joining hackathons, and reading/writing about tech.

Nards Paragas — 2021
